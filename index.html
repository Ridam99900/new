FAQ'S

Who can participate in Teknovation?

Only Senior Wing students of Sheiling House School are eligible to enroll and participate in this competition. It is an inter-house competition exclusively for Sheilingites.

How can I participate?

If you are a Senior Wing Sheilingite, you can participate by checking the details of your house in the Teams section. Report to your team leader or teacher during the 0 period at school.

Is there any participation fee?

No, Teknovation is completely free for all Sheilingites.

Do I need to bring my own supplies for the competition?

Yes, you need to bring your own technical devices or any equipment required for your respective competition. The committee will not provide any devices.

Where can I find more details about the event?

You can find all the detailed information about the event in the brochure provided here.

What are the consequences of cheating?

Cheating can lead to direct elimination from the event, and your team may suffer a significant point deduction.

What should I have during the event?

1. Participant Badge : Carry your Participant Badge at all times. Being found without it around the campus can result in strict actions against your team. 2. Accessories : Ensure you have all necessary accessories if you are participating in a tech event.

What is Teknovation?

Teknovation is the annual event organized by SHCC to challenge the houses of Sheiling House School in a competitive environment. This event tests your talents, skills, and mental power.

What will the winners of Teknovation receive?

The winning team will be awarded a trophy and certificates for team winners . Additionally, they will earn the title of “Champions of Teknovation” for the year 2024.
